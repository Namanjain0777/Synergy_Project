Uses

  • Assistive Technology: Provides mobility solutions for individuals with limited hand movement or disabilities, allowing them to control vehicles or wheelchairs through simple gestures.
  • Industrial Automation: Enables hands-free control of machinery in manufacturing environments, improving safety and efficiency in situations where traditional controls may be impractical.
  • Search and Rescue Operations: Facilitates the navigation of small, agile robots in disaster areas or confined spaces, allowing rescuers to control the devices remotely with intuitive gestures.
  • Interactive Exhibitions: Enhances museum or science center displays by allowing visitors to control exhibit elements through gestures, creating an engaging and immersive learning experience.
  • Drone Control: Offers an alternative method for piloting drones, potentially simplifying the control process and making drone operation more accessible to novice users.
